Managing Trustees

K K Raja

Founder

KK Raja, the founder and the source of inspiration behind the Latha Raja Foundation, is a Civil Engineer, promoter of Indian Art and Culture, Event Manager and above all a hope of the hopeless. He was the Founder Secretary of Chaitanya Art Theatres. His vision and mission and eternal love for art and culture made the Chaitanya Art Theatres as one of the leading cultural organizations in Telangana and AP with branches in Vijayawada and Vishakapatnam.
He organised 1700 events under the banner of Chaitanya Art Theatres for the past 30 years which include memorable events like 10-day 'Magicania', a Magic and Musc Festival, 31-day cultural festival of 'Nelaninda Vennala' and 'Kala Kiranalu-20', a cultural fest where 20 cultural programmes run at same time on same day in 20 theatres in Hyderabad.
For the past 8 years, through Exotic Events & Promotions Pvt Ltd., an one stop resource for total Event Management solutionm Mr. Raja had organised many notable and praiseworthy events like 'Octave', a 3-day northeastern state folk festival. He had worked as an event manager for several State Governament events in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh for Department of Culture, Language, Agriculture etc., where dignitaries like Chief Ministers of the State, Governors, Central Ministers and State Ministers attended as honorable guests.
