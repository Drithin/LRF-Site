Amma Nanna Anaada Ashramam
Telangana

Amma Nanna Anaada Ashramam is an India non-profit organisation tackling problems of needy, mentally challenged and underprivileged sections and support them by giving aid along with lodging and boarding. Sanksriti Samvardhan Mandal
Maharastra

Sanksriti Samvardhan Mandal (SSM) has been active in integrated rural development since 1959. Started with a small school, SSM has now grown into a 200 acre development hub. The focus of its activities has always been on the deprived rural communities, especially women and girls. SSM has been working in 10 blocks of Nanded and Beed districts of Maharastra and changed lives of 3000 families through its multi dimensional acitivities. It has a vision to Educate and Empower the rural communities.

Lebenshilfe
Andhra Pradesh

In German, Lebenshilfe means Life Help. At Lebenshilfe India, intellectually impaired children from the poorest segments of the society are becomning happy, independent adults through a unique synthesis of special education methods from around the world. Specialized training in local folk arts and a distinctive program using arts as therapy. After studying Special Education in America, Lebenshilfe India Founder Ms Saraswati Devi took intensive training at Lebenshilfe, Germany. On her return to India, with support of iriginal organisation, she found it in India in 1980.

Karnataka

At start of day, Mr Raja and his tram from the home scout for the distressed poor humans in need of immediate help and bring them in.
